About Sitegyan What we are upto, Powernote and more

What we are upto

Traditional Web Collaboration and Management tools have very less to do with the Website itself. Our attempt at Sitegyan is to bridge that gap and bring it as close as possible to the web developers. End users or peers can attach a JS snippet to their home template to activate Powernote and can start adding notes by double clicking on the frame where potential bugs/ suggestions are valid and the other developers would come to know immediately.

Read more on our blog

Browser support

Powernote is tested and supported in major modern browsers like Chrome and Firefox.

  • Latest Google Chrome
  • Firefox 4+

Features

These are the features that Powernote has

  • Create Projects and add people
  • Add notes directly on your web application
  • Raise tickets directly from these notes and assign people
  • Compare notes/ tickets/ suggestions with the admin
  • Create notes out of chat trascripts so you dont have to create ticket
